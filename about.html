Supermilk is a small creative studio in Toronto’s Chinatown. We focus on storytelling through the power of art & design.

We’ve worked with start-ups and big companies on websites, publications, branding and data driven tools. Using our industry skills, we are interested in creating educational resources meant to teach people about the world. Climate change is the #1 issue of our generation and we want to do our part to get everyone on the same page.

Take a peek at our portfolio online and follow us on Instagram for more content.

upatfive.ca instagram

Up at Five is a digital studio focused on data driven application development. Our work is centred around harnessing the unique ability of data as a story telling device. We love exploring new ideas and collaborating on projects that challenge users to see the world through a new lense. We also really love the internet.

You can find us on the internet @up_at_five.
